## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Two-category structure

The Manfaat Penelitian section SHALL separate benefits into two categories: Manfaat Teoritis (theoretical) and Manfaat Praktis (practical).

#### Scenario: Category headings

- **WHEN** the section is structured
- **THEN** it contains subsections or numbered groups for teoritis and praktis

### Requirement: Two theoretical benefits

Manfaat Teoritis SHALL include:

1. Contribution to body of knowledge on low-code agentic framework design patterns
2. Reference architecture for combining declarative paradigm with agent communication standards

#### Scenario: Academic contribution

- **WHEN** theoretical benefits are stated
- **THEN** they address knowledge contribution to AI/multi-agent systems field

### Requirement: Three practical benefits

Manfaat Praktis SHALL include:

1. For SRE practitioners: accessible tool for building observability agents without programming
2. For organizations: reduced barrier to adopting agentic automation
3. For UNY/academic community: reference implementation for further research

#### Scenario: Stakeholder coverage

- **WHEN** practical benefits are listed
- **THEN** they identify specific beneficiary groups (practitioners, organizations, institution)

### Requirement: Benefit-objective traceability

Each benefit SHALL relate to at least one research objective from Tujuan Penelitian.

#### Scenario: Objective linkage

- **WHEN** practical benefit for SRE practitioners is stated
- **THEN** it traces to objective 3 (implementation and validation)

### Requirement: Numbered sub-list format

Each category SHALL use numbered list for individual benefits.

#### Scenario: Format compliance

- **WHEN** benefits are formatted
- **THEN** teoritis has 2 numbered items and praktis has 3 numbered items
